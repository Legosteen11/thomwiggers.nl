About me

I am a student at the Radboud University in Nijmegen, studying Computer Security in the TRU/e Master programme. This programme is a collaboration between the Radboud University and the Technical University of Eindhoven (TU/e). I hold Bachelor's degrees in both Computing Science and Information Sciences, both from Radboud University.

Currently I am a web developer for InQbator B.V.. For them, I am working on a Django webapplication. I'm also a teaching assistent at Radboud University for the undergraduate Functional Programming course, teaching students how to program in Clean and Haskell (I like Haskell better) and grading their homework. I've also assisted the Operating Systems course, grading homework and giving lectures (werkcolleges) about the assignments.

Since September 2014 I represent the students of Computing Science on the board of the Education Institute for Computing and Information Sciences (OII) as Assessor Informatica.

In my spare time I have been involved with ICT Netwerk Nijmegen. I've also been on the board of Studievereniging Thalia and still a part of various committees. I also occasionally contribute to various open-source projects, mostly when I run into something and have to fix it for myself. See also my Github profile. I also have a profile on Stack Overflow.

You can also find me on LinkedIn and Twitter.
